About

Holcombe L. Rucker High School is a school based in the South Bronx. Our character and foundation are based upon four words: Community, Commitment, Collaboration, & Confidence.

Community: Promoting inclusivity, facilitating open communication, and providing clear expectations to all members of the community to ensure a healthy, productive environment, inside and outside the school.

Commitment: Each student possesses a commitment to excellence in every area of life, possessing a level of maturity that enables them to entrust themselves with the knowledge we instill in them.

Collaboration: Encouraging creative problem-solving in dynamic groups where outcomes are not always predictable, but ultimately greater than any individual perspective could envision.

Confidence: Nothing succeeds like confidence. We develop activities where students can shine, and will want to continue the positive feelings generated by that confidence and persue career and life objectives.

It is our mission to promote high expectations for students in all areas: academic, artistic, physical, health, citizenship and service. We will commit the resources necessary to establish and maintain:

A respectful, caring and safe environment.
Students centered instruction based on researchInformation driven decision making.
A challenging curriculum emphasizing community research.
Collaboration with outside partners to enhance student learning.
An active partnership with parents and the community.
