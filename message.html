Principal’s Message

Welcome to the 2015-2016 Holcombe L. Rucker High School of Community Research School Year

Welcome to our school community website! At Rucker High School, our educators and staff work hard to ensure that your students will become informed leaders and reliable decision makers. Our students strive for excellent attendance, graduation and Regents completion rates. High school is a miniature version of the real world, but we provide strong safety nets where students will learn the necessary skills to navigate life with increasing success and confidence.

Our instructional programs and campus life promote an environment where our students are less reactive and increasingly proactive - driven towards becoming solutions oriented. By the end of their high school experience at Holcombe L. Rucker School of Community Research, we expect our student to become college and career ready with their own plans for leading and sustaining healthy, productive lives.

We are a community school that is supported by a vibrant community and excellent community partners. I urge you to visit us during an open house or schedule an appointment to meet with us to discuss your secondary, college and career goals. Please contact our parent coordinator, Devin Carthan, to request further information and schedule an appointment. Please see the parent coordinator link on the Resources for Parents page for his contact information.

We will continue to journal the wonderful things that are happening here, so please check back soon to see more of the exciting moments that our students are creating here at Holcombe L. Rucker High School. Thank you for visiting our window to the world. See you soon!

Sincerely,

Sharif Rucker
